Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ErgoVision.

How does the posture monitoring work?

expand_more

Our application uses your device's camera and Google's MediaPipe Pose Landmark AI to analyze your posture in real-time. It detects key body points to determine if you are slouching or in a suboptimal position, providing immediate feedback to help you correct it.

Is my camera data stored or shared?

expand_more

Your privacy is our top priority. All pose detection and analysis happen locally on your device. We do not record, store, or transmit your camera feed. Your data never leaves your computer.

What devices are compatible?

expand_more

ErgoVision is a web-based application that works on any modern desktop or laptop with a webcam and an up-to-date web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Mobile device support is also available.

Can I customize the alerts?

expand_more

Yes! You can customize the sensitivity of the posture detection and the type of notifications you receive. We offer visual and audio alerts, and you can adjust them to fit your preferences and work environment.
